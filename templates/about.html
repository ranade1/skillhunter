What this is all about

Skillhunter is all about helping you and your mates identify the most in-demand skills in the job market in order to find a new job as soon as possible. Cut the bullshit and prepare yourself in the most efficient way, no need to learn everything!

Just try to search something like python developer and explore skills ranked by their frequency, where a number indicates how often companies call a particular skill.

For example, entering python developer and after a short wait (for popular positions the process may take up to several minutes) you will get a result similar to the following:

Python – 747
Django – 239
Git – 154
Docker – 145
PostgreSQL – 138
...
